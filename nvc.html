The Four Components of NVC

Nonviolent Communication (NVC), developed by Marshall Rosenberg, is a powerful framework for conflict resolution and genuine connection. It guides us to express ourselves and listen to others with empathy by focusing on four key steps:

  • **Observation:** Stating the facts without judgment.
  • **Feeling:** Expressing how we feel in relation to what we observe.
  • **Need:** Identifying the universal human needs that are met or unmet.
  • **Request:** Asking for concrete actions to enrich life.
